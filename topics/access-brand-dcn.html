Accessing the brand-dcn Skill and Plugin

Written July 2, 2026 v1.0 Covers brand-dcn plugin 1.0.0

brand-dcn puts our brand voice and the full marketing asset library inside Claude. With it installed, anything you write for doctors and their teams comes out sounding like one company, with the right colors, logos, fonts, and boilerplate already in hand.

Why

Why you would use this

Before this skill, staying on brand meant hunting through SharePoint for the current logo, the approved boilerplate, or the right template, and hoping the copy you found was the latest version. brand-dcn bundles a snapshot of the entire Brand Assets library, so Claude already knows our voice, our tagline, our palette, our logo rules, and our approved numbers when you sit down to write.

That matters for two reasons. First, consistency: a doctor who reads our emails, our sell sheets, and our social posts should hear the same humble, kind, confident voice everywhere. Second, speed: you spend your time on what the piece needs to say, and Claude handles how we say it. It is also a gentle on-ramp for newer team members, who can write like DCN from their first week.

How

How you set it up and use it

Getting it installed

  1. brand-dcn is distributed through our internal plugin catalog in Claude, managed by IT. For most of the team it installs automatically, so start by checking: open Claude and ask it "do you have the brand-dcn skill?"
  2. If it is not there, open your Claude settings, find Plugins (or Capabilities), and look for brand-dcn in the dentalcrafters catalog. Install it from there.
  3. If you do not see the catalog at all, contact IT and we will sort out your access.

Using it day to day

  1. Most of the time you do nothing special. When you ask Claude to write or review anything doctor facing or customer facing, the skill triggers on its own and applies the brand rules.
  2. You can also call it directly: type /brand-dcn, or ask in plain language, for example "make this on-brand" or "rewrite this in our voice."
  3. Ask it brand questions too: which logo to use on a dark slide, our exact hex colors, the tagline, or the About Us boilerplate. It answers from the bundled guidelines rather than memory.

Staying current

Marketing owns the master files and ships updates as new plugin versions, which reach you automatically through the catalog. If something in the skill looks out of date, tell Marketing so they can publish a refresh; please do not swap in files from other locations yourself, since the bundled snapshot is what keeps the whole team consistent.

When

When you might use it

Reach for brand-dcn whenever a piece will be seen by doctors, their teams, or the public:

  • Doctor and practice emails, proposals, and case follow-ups
  • Sell sheets, one-pagers, presentations, and event or CE promos
  • Web copy, landing pages, social posts, and newsletters
  • Branding or cleaning up a document someone else started
  • Any question about our voice, colors, fonts, logos, or templates

And when it is not the right tool: purely internal writing (like IT documentation or this site's plumbing) does not need the brand lens, quality-system and QMS questions have their own skill, and the current month's content calendar should come from Marketing directly, since the bundled copy is a format example rather than the live calendar.
